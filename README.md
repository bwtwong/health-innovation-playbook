# Health Commercialisation & Innovation Playbook
## A Living Framework Repository

The frameworks that govern how a health innovation actually reaches patients — readiness scales, regulatory pathways, reimbursement mechanics, and go-to-market strategy.

**Live site:** `https://bwtwong.github.io/health-innovation-playbook` *(update once deployed)*

Part of a series alongside [The Consulting Playbook](https://bwtwong.github.io/consulting-playbook), the [Impact Investing Playbook](https://bwtwong.github.io/impact-investing-playbook), and the [Implementation Science & MEL Cheatsheet](https://bwtwong.github.io/implementation-science-cheatsheet). See the full series at the [notebook hub](https://bwtwong.github.io/notebooks).

## Important note

This is a practitioner's learning notebook, **not regulatory or legal advice**. TGA requirements, PBAC/MSAC processes, and reimbursement mechanics change — always verify current requirements directly with TGA, the Department of Health and Aged Care, and a qualified regulatory consultant before acting.
